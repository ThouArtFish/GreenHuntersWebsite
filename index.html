Who are the Green Hunters?

We are a non-profitable group funded by the Young Ealing
Foundation that believes every child has the right to play in a
clean and safe environment.

How do you become a Green Hunter?

All you need to do is turn up at the events listed in the display
below and help out with cleaning up the location!
You should also check out this document so you can participate safely in the event!

What do I need to become a Green Hunter?

If you are under 13, ask a parent or carer for
permission or maybe get them to come along!
Equipment for litter picking will be given out during the event
which must be returned, but we advise bringing a
water bottle and a snack to keep you energised!
